# May 2026 Content Plan v1

Project: `moltyflywheel.com`  
Cadence: `1 post / day`  
Month: `May 2026`  
Primary operating rule: SEO-first, English-first, decision-supportive content only

## Purpose

This plan defines the daily content queue for May 2026.

Goals:
- maintain daily publishing rhythm after the April launch batch
- deepen topical authority around live tool pages and high-intent clusters
- strengthen internal links into `Tools`, `Programs`, `Offers`, and existing blog pillars
- expand commercial-support coverage without turning the blog into generic list content

## Planning logic

May should not repeat the April pattern exactly.

April covered:
- Agentic AI foundational layer
- Claude AI layer
- AI Flywheel / business model layer
- Higgsfield support cluster
- selected news analysis

May should focus on:
1. `Abacus AI` support cluster
2. `Topview AI` support cluster
3. decision-support content for `Tools` and `Offers`
4. program and workflow commercial-support content
5. weekly news / flex posts only where the topic is worth the slot

- supports `/programs/*`

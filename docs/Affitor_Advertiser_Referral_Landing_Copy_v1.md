## The Real Problem

Most teams do not fail because affiliate growth “does not work.”  
They fail because the system around it is messy.

Common friction points look like this:

- partner tracking is unclear
- attribution becomes messy fast
- onboarding affiliates is inconsistent
- commission logic is hard to explain and harder to operate
- payouts become manual work
- the whole thing lives in spreadsheets, DMs, and disconnected tools

That is usually where momentum dies.

If your team is small, or your growth stack is still forming, building this from scratch is often not the highest-leverage move.

## Why Affitor Is Worth Looking At

Affitor is worth evaluating if your goal is not just “get affiliate links live,” but actually build a workable partner channel.

What makes it relevant is the operational side:

- a more structured advertiser onboarding path

- less manual glue work compared with fully improvised workflows
- better fit for teams that want partner growth to become part of the system, not just a side experiment

The real value is not “one more platform.”

- launching a program

- paying out fairly
- keeping the whole thing understandable for both your team and your partners

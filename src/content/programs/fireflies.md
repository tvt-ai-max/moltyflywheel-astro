## What Is Fireflies?

Fireflies is an AI meeting assistant that records, transcribes, summarizes, and makes meeting knowledge searchable across team workflows.

Key features:
- **Meeting transcription** — capture conversations from Zoom, Meet, Teams, and other tools
- **AI summaries** — convert long meetings into action items and concise notes
- **Searchable meeting memory** — find past decisions and moments quickly
- **Broad integrations** — works with conferencing, CRM, and workflow tools
- **Free tier** — teams can test it deeply before committing

## Who Is It For?

Fireflies is best for remote teams, agencies, consultants, sales teams, and operators who spend enough time in meetings that transcription quickly becomes a workflow requirement instead of a nice-to-have.

It also fits creator-operator audiences who want better meeting capture, research recall, and internal documentation without building a manual notes system.

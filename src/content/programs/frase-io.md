## What Is Frase.io?

Frase.io is an AI-powered SEO content research and optimization platform. It helps content teams and SEO professionals create search-optimized articles by analyzing the top-ranking pages for any keyword and generating comprehensive content briefs automatically.

Where Surfer SEO focuses on on-page optimization scoring, Frase.io emphasizes research depth — pulling in the questions people ask (from Reddit, Quora, and People Also Ask), competitor outlines, and topic coverage gaps.

Key features:
- **SERP research** — instant analysis of top 20 results for any keyword
- **Content briefs** — automatically generated outlines based on competitor coverage
- **AI writer** — generate full articles directly inside Frase with your brief as context
- **Answer engine** — shows exactly what questions your target audience is asking online
- **Content optimization** — topic score to track how well your content covers the subject
- **Content analytics** — integrates with GSC to track which articles need refreshing
- **Team collaboration** — shared briefs and workspaces for content teams

## Who Is It For?

Frase.io is ideal for content teams producing research-heavy articles, SEO agencies managing multiple clients, and bloggers who want to outrank competitors on informational keywords. Plans start at $45/month, making it accessible to solo content creators.

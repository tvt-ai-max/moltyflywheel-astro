## What Is Surfer SEO?

Surfer SEO is an on-page content optimization platform that analyzes the top-ranking pages for any keyword and generates a data-backed brief for your content. It tells you exactly how many words to write, which terms to include, how many headings to use, and what images to add — all based on what's actually ranking.

Key features:
- **Content Editor** — real-time SEO scoring as you write, showing keyword density, structure, and NLP terms
- **SERP Analyzer** — deep competitive analysis of any keyword
- **Audit tool** — identify why existing pages aren't ranking and fix them
- **Keyword Research** — topic clusters and keyword opportunities
- **AI Writer integration** — generate SEO-optimized drafts in the Content Editor
- **Google Docs & WordPress integration** — write where you're comfortable

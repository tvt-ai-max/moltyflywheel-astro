SEMrush is the Swiss Army knife of digital marketing platforms, and their affiliate program (run through Impact) is one of the highest-paying in the SEO space. With $200 per paid conversion and $10 per free trial, even moderate traffic can generate substantial affiliate income.

## What Is SEMrush?

SEMrush is a comprehensive digital marketing suite covering SEO, PPC advertising, content marketing, social media, and competitive research. It's used by over 10 million marketers worldwide, including teams at major brands like eBay, Samsung, and Booking.com.

Core capabilities:
- **Keyword research** — 25+ billion keywords in the database
- **Site audit** — automated technical SEO crawling and issue detection
- **Competitive intelligence** — analyze any competitor's traffic, keywords, and backlinks
- **Backlink analytics** — comprehensive link profile analysis
- **Position tracking** — daily rank monitoring for your target keywords
- **Content marketing toolkit** — SEO writing assistant, topic research, post tracking
- **PPC research** — analyze competitor Google Ads strategies

## Who Is It For?

SEMrush targets SEO professionals, digital marketing agencies, and content teams. Plans start at $139/month, which means your $200 commission per sale is nearly 1.5x the first month's payment — SEMrush invests heavily in acquiring customers through affiliates.

## Commission Details

SEMrush runs one of the most generous hybrid commission structures in digital marketing:
- **$200 per paid subscription** (one-time, not recurring)
- **$10 per free trial activation** (even if they don't convert to paid)
- **120-day cookie duration** — the longest of any major SEO tool

The high cookie duration and dual payout structure make SEMrush particularly attractive for content sites where visitors research tools over weeks before buying.

## Pros

- $200 flat commission is among the highest in SEO tools
- $10 per trial means low-intent visitors still generate revenue
- 120-day cookie is exceptional — one of the best in the industry
- Massive brand recognition; little convincing needed
- Dedicated affiliate team and promotional materials

## Cons

- One-time commission (not recurring) — compare to Surfer's 25% recurring before deciding priority
- $139+/month price point narrows your potential audience to serious marketers
- High competition in the affiliate space for SEMrush reviews

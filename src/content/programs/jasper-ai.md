## What Is Jasper AI?

Jasper AI is a generative AI writing platform designed for marketing teams who need brand-consistent content at scale. Originally launched as Jarvis.ai in 2021, it was one of the first mainstream AI writing tools and has since pivoted toward enterprise customers with features like brand voice training and multi-user collaboration.

Key features:
- **Brand Voice** — train Jasper on your brand's tone, terminology, and style guidelines
- **Campaigns** — generate all marketing assets for a campaign simultaneously (emails, social posts, ads, blog posts)
- **Templates** — 50+ pre-built templates for specific content types
- **Jasper Art** — AI image generation integrated into the platform
- **Team collaboration** — multi-seat accounts with role permissions
- **Chrome extension** — write with Jasper anywhere on the web
- **Integrations** — Surfer SEO, Webflow, Google Docs, and more

## Who Is It For?

Jasper AI targets marketing managers, content teams at funded startups, and agencies. The Creator plan starts at $49/month, making it accessible to individual marketers, while the Pro and Business plans ($69–$499/month) serve growing teams.

## Commission Details

Jasper's affiliate program pays **25% recurring commission** on all active subscriptions. The 45-day cookie is above average for the AI writing space. Monthly payouts are processed via PayPal through their Impact affiliate platform.

At $49/month Creator plan, you earn $12.25/month per referral. At Pro ($69/month), that's $17.25/month. Jasper's enterprise-tier customers can drive significantly higher commissions.

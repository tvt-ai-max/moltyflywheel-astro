## What Is Lovable?

Lovable is an AI-powered full-stack app builder that turns plain-English prompts into working web applications with frontend, backend, database setup, and deployment paths.

Key features:
- **Prompt-to-app workflow** — describe an app idea and generate a working build quickly
- **Full-stack output** — frontend, backend, and database layers handled in one system
- **Free entry path** — enough for early experimentation before paying
- **Fast MVP building** — useful for founders, freelancers, and internal-tool builders

## Who Is It For?

Lovable is a fit for non-technical founders, indie hackers, freelancers building MVPs, and operators who want to prototype apps quickly without hiring a traditional development team upfront.

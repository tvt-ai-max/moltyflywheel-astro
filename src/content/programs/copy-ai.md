## What Is Copy.ai?

Copy.ai is an AI-powered marketing workflow platform that helps teams automate repetitive copywriting tasks at scale. Unlike blog-focused tools like KoalaWriter, Copy.ai specializes in short-form marketing copy: ad copy, email subject lines, product descriptions, social media captions, and sales pages.

Recent versions have introduced "Workflows" — automated multi-step pipelines that can generate entire content systems from a single input. For example: input a product URL, and Copy.ai can generate a full campaign including ad copy, email sequence, and social posts automatically.

Key features:
- **Workflows** — multi-step automation for content generation at scale
- **90+ copywriting templates** — structured prompts for every marketing use case
- **Brand Voice** — company-specific tone and style settings
- **Infobase** — store company knowledge for consistent AI output
- **Team collaboration** — shared workspaces and asset libraries
- **API access** — programmatic content generation for developers

## Who Is It For?

## What Is Notion AI?

Notion AI is an AI writing and productivity assistant built into the Notion workspace platform. It allows users to generate content, summarize notes, translate text, create action items, and more — all without leaving their existing Notion workspace.

Notion itself is an all-in-one workspace that replaces Confluence, Asana, Google Docs, and Airtable for many teams. Notion AI is an add-on that costs $10/month per user, layered on top of existing Notion plans.

Key AI features:
- **AI writing assistant** — generate, expand, rewrite, and edit text in any Notion document
- **Autofill database properties** — automatically populate structured data using AI
- **AI summaries** — instant summaries of long pages and meeting notes
- **Smart search** — semantic search across your entire Notion workspace
- **Q&A** — ask questions about your workspace content and get instant answers
- **Translation** — translate content into 14 languages

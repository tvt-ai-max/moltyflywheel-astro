Writesonic is a versatile AI content platform that covers everything from blog posts to Google Ads copy and ecommerce product descriptions. Their affiliate program offers 30% recurring commission, making it a decent addition to an AI writing program portfolio.

## What Is Writesonic?

Writesonic is an AI writing tool that spans multiple content types: long-form blog articles, landing pages, social media posts, Google and Facebook ad copy, and ecommerce product descriptions. It's powered by GPT-4 and Claude API, with recent expansion into an AI chatbot (Chatsonic) and an AI search product (Botsonic).

Key features:
- **Article Writer** — long-form blog post generation with SEO optimization
- **Chatsonic** — AI chatbot with real-time web search (like ChatGPT with browsing)
- **Botsonic** — custom chatbot builder for websites
- **Landing page generator** — conversion-optimized pages from a brief
- **Ad copy tools** — Google Ads, Facebook Ads, LinkedIn Ad generators
- **Ecommerce product descriptions** — bulk generation for online stores
- **Paraphrasing and editing tools** — rewrite and enhance existing content

## Who Is It For?

Writesonic targets freelancers, content marketers, digital agencies, and ecommerce businesses that need a broad range of content types from a single tool. Plans start at $16/month for basic use, with more powerful options at $79/month+.

## Commission Details

Writesonic's affiliate program pays **30% recurring commission** through their First Promoter platform. The 30-day cookie is on the shorter side. Payouts are processed monthly via PayPal with a $50 minimum threshold.

## Pros

- Wide range of content types increases your promotion angles
- 30% recurring commission is competitive
- Free trial available — lowers conversion barrier
- Chatsonic/Botsonic add product depth beyond basic AI writing

## Cons

- 30-day cookie is shorter than most competitors (KoalaWriter: 60 days, ConvertKit: 90 days)
- Brand awareness is lower than Jasper AI or Copy.ai in most markets
- Product quality is inconsistent compared to Claude-native or GPT-4 native tools
- Churning more aggressively than premium alternatives as users upgrade to better tools

ConvertKit (now rebranded as Kit) is the email marketing platform of choice for independent creators, bloggers, and online business owners. Their affiliate program is one of the most generous in the industry: 30% recurring commission for the lifetime of the customer, with a 90-day cookie window.

## What Is ConvertKit?

ConvertKit is a creator-focused email marketing platform that combines email automation, landing pages, forms, and a digital product marketplace into one clean interface. Unlike MailChimp (which was built for e-commerce bulk email), ConvertKit was designed from the ground up for audience-building and monetization.

Key features:
- **Visual automation builder** — create complex email sequences with a drag-and-drop canvas
- **Subscriber tagging and segmentation** — personalize sequences based on behavior
- **Landing pages and opt-in forms** — built-in lead capture without needing extra tools
- **Commerce features** — sell digital products and courses directly via ConvertKit
- **Creator Network** — cross-promotion with other creators to grow your list
- **Free plan** — up to 1,000 subscribers, making it easy for new creators to start

## Who Is It For?

ConvertKit is ideal for bloggers, podcasters, newsletter creators, coaches, and anyone building an audience online. Their free tier is a major conversion driver — once creators hit 1,000 subscribers and need automation features, they upgrade to paid plans starting at $29/month.

## Commission Details

The ConvertKit affiliate program pays **30% recurring commission** for the lifetime of the customer — not just year one. With plans ranging from $29/month to $2,500+/month for large lists, your commissions scale with your referrals' success.

The 90-day cookie is exceptional — one of the longest in the email marketing space. Payouts are processed monthly via PayPal, with a $50 minimum threshold.

## Pros

- **Lifetime recurring commissions** — rare in the industry; your income grows over years, not months
- 90-day cookie gives you maximum conversion time
- Free plan makes it easy for new audiences to try the product (higher conversion rate)
- Strong product-market fit: creators don't leave once they're set up on ConvertKit
- Dedicated affiliate manager and resources available

## Cons

- Lower plan prices ($29/month) mean your early commissions are modest (~$8.70/month per referral)
- Primarily a North American market product; less penetration in Southeast Asia
- Competitive space — many other affiliates promote ConvertKit, so you need strong content to stand out

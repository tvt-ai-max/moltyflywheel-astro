The single most important filter for building a sustainable affiliate income is this: recurring commissions. A one-time $200 payout sounds attractive — but a 30% recurring commission on a $100/month SaaS tool that your referral keeps for 3 years earns you $1,080 from a single customer.

In the AI tools space, recurring affiliate programs are more common than in almost any other niche. Here are the seven best, ranked by long-term income potential.

## Why Recurring Commissions Change the Math

Before the list: let's understand why recurring commissions are so much more valuable than one-time payouts.

**One-time commission example:**
- Refer 10 customers this month → Earn $2,000 (at $200/conversion)
- Next month, you need to refer 10 more customers to earn another $2,000
- Income is entirely dependent on this month's traffic

**Recurring commission example:**
- Refer 10 customers this month → Earn $300 (at $30/month commission)
- Month 2: Same 10 customers still subscribed → Earn another $300 (no new traffic needed)
- Month 12: 10 original + 100 new customers → Earn $3,300/month

Recurring commissions build an asset that generates income independent of your current traffic levels. This is the foundation of the MoltyFlywheel income model.
